About Us

CCAP Ekwendeni Lay Training Centre is one of the departments of the Church of Central Africa Presbyterian Synod of Livingstonia. The Centre focuses on empowering individuals through Christian leadership development, vocational skills training and community development programs.

The Centre also provides conference facilities and quality accommodation for individuals, churches, organisations and community groups.

We are located at Ekwendeni in Mzimba District, Malawi.

Vision

To become a leading Christian Training Centre.

Mission

To provide holistic leadership development, vocational skills training, conference facilities and quality accommodation.

Training Programs

Christian Leadership Development

Training church leaders, youth leaders and community leaders with biblical and practical leadership skills.

Vocational Skills Training

Our vocational programs equip students with practical skills for employment and self-reliance.

  • Carpentry and Joinery
  • Tailoring and Fashion Design
  • Cosmetology (Hair Dressing)
  • Electrical Installation and Electronics
  • Plumbing
  • Soap Making
  • Mobile Phone Repair
  • Edible Horticulture
  • Seed Production
